About

I'm a web developer of 7 years with a passion for UX and interaction. I love delivering content in new and interesting ways and solving business problems with innovative solutions.

My focus is typically on the front end with extensive experience in HTML5, CSS3, and JavaScript. I also get busy on the back end with PHP, and MySQL. Like any efficient developer I leverage frameworks such as AngularJS, Backbone, Laravel, and Wordpress. NodeJS, canvas, and TweenMax are my new areas of interest.

Currently residing in Austin, TX, I'm a huge fan of live music and bbq. I guess I'm in the right place! Health, fitness and travel are some of my other passions in life.

TQ Taco Tour with Food Porn

To refresh the TQ Taco Tour for SXSW 2015, we added a food porn experience. From a mobile device, users could upload short videos of themselves enjoying the tacos on the tour. Using FFMPEG on the server, the video was slowed down, a vintage filter was applied, then some custom taco porn music was added. All this resulted in some funny video clips that were uploaded to our Youtube channel. Beanstalkd queues were used to coordinate the heavy video processing and the Youtube API uploads.
